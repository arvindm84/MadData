What is MadData?

MadData is a civic-tech data platform that bridges urban planners and entrepreneurs. We integrate City of Madison zoning and parcel data with hyper-local sentiment from Yelp and Google Reviews to identify "Retail Deserts"—areas where community demand is unmet.

The result: you can see where the gaps are on an interactive map, or describe your business idea and get recommendations for the best places to open—so the next shop on State Street is exactly what the community has been waiting for.

How to Use the App

You can use MadData in two ways: explore the map to see where opportunities are, or use the Venture Matchmaker to describe your business idea and get location recommendations.

1

Explore the Interactive Map

Our geospatial heatmap shows current vacancies and Retail Deserts across Madison. You can see where demand is high and supply is low at a glance.

  1. Open the Map page.
  2. Use the heatmap to find vacant storefronts and Retail Deserts.
  3. Explore neighborhood context to identify high-probability areas.
